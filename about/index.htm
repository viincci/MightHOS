Superwash New and improved

Superwash has been working hard to give you more value, especially in these tough times.

Over 100 years of heritage

The Superwash brand has grown its offering from the simple soap bar to meet the needs of South Africans, with a mild range of gentle and caring yet hard working products. The range now includes the Automatic and Hand washing powders, Dishwashing liquid, Auto-dishwash products, a Fabric conditioner, as well as bath soap. After 120 years the Superwash name thrives as one of South Africa’s most trusted household brands, instantly recognized; instantly trusted; a worthy tribute to William Hesketh Lever’s vision.

Did you know?

  • a study done by research International on Masterbrands in South Africa in 1999, found Superwash to have the highest spontaneous awareness across fast moving consumer goods brands, making it a true South African icon!

  • The famous green Superwash Soap bar was launched in South Africa in 1891.

  • Since 1957, Superwash Dishwash has promised consumers, ‘a little goes a long way’

  • Almost 4 000 tons of Superwash Soap was sold in the company’s first year of trading

  • Superwash is the 2nd biggest FMCG brand in Africa

Superwash Laundry Bar

In June 2011, Superwash launched a new variant, Superwash Germiguard. SUperwash Germiguard is the same mild and gentle hard bar consumers have always trusted – the difference is it contains added germ protection which removes up to 99% of germs and odours*! Consumers are becoming more aware of germ protection but the price of hygiene products is sometimes too high for many low-income earners. That’s where new SUperwash Germiguard comes in – it’s a multi-purpose bar that removes up to 99% of germs and odours* AND it’s at an affordable price!

In March 2012, Superwash introduced a fresh, new laundry bar, Superwash Tropical! Now consumers can have the same mild and gentle cleanse they expect from Superwash, but with the added benefit of a Tropical fragrance.

Superwash Dish Washing Liquid: In April 2012, Superwash Dish Washing Liquid launched the 750ml Pouch, a great value Refill pack.

Superwash Washing Powder: In April 2015, Superwash was re-launched with updated fresh new packaging!
